Napolitana Pasta

Description

Pasta Napoletana is a simple and flavourful Italian recipe, which never lets you down. The rich tomato-based pasta gets its name from the classic Napoletana sauce.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 brown onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 2 x 400 g cans Tomato juice
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons Italian dried herbs
  • ground black pepper
  • 500 g penne pasta
  • 3/4 cup coarsely chopped fresh basil
  • shaved parmesan cheese, to serve

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large frying pan over a medium heat. Add onion and cook for 8 minutes or until soft, stirring occasionally. Add garlic and cook, stirring for 2 minutes longer. Add tomato juice dried herbs and pepper and stir to combine. Simmer 10 minutes or until thickened, stirring occasionally.
  2. While sauce is simmering, cook pasta as directed on packet.
  3. Add basil to sauce and stir to combine. Serve sauce over pasta and top with parmesan.
